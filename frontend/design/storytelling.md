# Storytelling (editorial scrollytelling)

Synced with repo-root `design.md`. TypeUI MCP source: `storytelling`.

## Editorial scrollytelling layer

- **Canvas:** `#0a0a0b` full-bleed sticky stage for charts and map
- **Foreground:** centered `st-card` panels — high-contrast light surface on dark scroll
- **Typography:** Abril Fatface (display), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (labels)
- **Chrome:** minimal borders; whitespace and typographic scale carry hierarchy
- **No doodle/sketch** effects, rotations, or hand-drawn borders
